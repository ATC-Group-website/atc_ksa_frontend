Tax services

ATC offers top quality tax services tailored to optimize your business's financial well-being.

Overview

ATC is proudly recognized as one of the premier providers of tax services in the MENA region, committed to enhancing the financial prosperity of your business. Our dedication to specialization is evident in our operational approach, with each department offering specialized expertise in distinct tax service areas.

Here are the types of tax services that ATC provide:

VAT Tax Return Consultancy Services:

  • Reviewing VAT returns based on provided documents and data to ensure compliance with the Value Added Tax system and its regulations for each tax period before submission to ZATCA (Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority).
  • Reviewing a sample of tax-deducted documents and providing feedback or recommendations.
  • Presenting key notes and recommendations before submitting the VAT return.
  • Offering technical support for submitting the return on the ZATCA website, requiring access to your "ZATCA login information."
  • Excluding technical support for tax examination requested by ZATCA (this service will be agreed upon separately if required).

Withholding Tax Return Consultancy Services :

  • Reviewing withholding tax returns based on the documents and data you provide, ensuring full compliance with the Income Tax system and its regulations before submission to the Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority (ZATCA).
  • Evaluating documents related to transactions with non-resident entities in Saudi Arabia that are subject to withholding tax and offering comprehensive feedback.
  • Providing technical support for implementing Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements between Saudi Arabia and non-resident countries, while excluding representation of the non-resident entity in dealings with ZATCA (a separate contract would be required for this service).
  • Offering detailed recommendations and technical assistance before submitting the withholding tax return on ZATCA's website, with access to your "ZATCA login information" for submission purposes.
  • Excluding technical support for tax examination requested by ZATCA (this service will be agreed upon separately if required).

Zakat Return Consultancy Services:

  • Reviewing Zakat returns and related documents to ensure compliance with Zakat regulations before submission to ZATCA.
  • Providing feedback on key financial items like revenues, costs, and assets, ensuring proper documentation.
  • Offering recommendations and technical support for submitting Zakat returns via ZATCA's website, with access to your login if needed.
  • Keeping you updated on Zakat regulations and instructions from ZATCA.
  • Excluding technical support for tax examination requested by ZATCA (this service will be agreed upon separately if required).

Income Tax Returns Consultancy Services:

  • Reviewing income tax returns and schedules for compliance with Income Tax regulations before submission to ZATCA.
  • Checking documents related to key financial items (revenues, costs, assets, liabilities) for proper documentation.
  • Excluding approval by a licensed auditor, which requires a separate contract if needed.
  • Providing notes, recommendations, and technical support for submitting returns via ZATCA's website, requiring login information if needed.
  • Excluding technical support for tax examination requested by ZATCA (this service will be agreed upon separately if required).

Auditing

Our Auditing Service provides insight into the financial health of organizations

Overview

We conduct thorough examinations of financial statements to ensure their integrity, instilling confidence in investors and capital markets. Within ATC's dynamic work environment, we also facilitate effective bookkeeping processes that align with regulatory frameworks. Our commitment is to provide comprehensive audit insights and robust evidence to support firms in their growth strategies and aspirations.

Our Team

All of our senior staff members are accredited accountants and auditors, possessing international qualifications or their local equivalent. Our professional teams bring extensive and diverse experience, having worked for prominent international accounting and auditing firms, as well as international and local entities across various sectors, including: Industrial, Commercial, Contracting, Construction, Real Estate, Tourism & Hotels, Telecommunication, Finance, Oil, Social Societies, and Service Industries.

Our Audit Approach:

  • Audit Planning

    Our team focuses on key audit areas, timely issue resolution, and efficient audit execution. This phase concludes with the identification of significant audit areas, their characteristics, audit timing, tailored procedures, and team selection.

  • Interim Audit

    During this phase, we assess the company's accounting system, regulatory compliance, and internal controls through document examination and testing. This evaluation guides the scope and approach of our year-end financial statement audit.

  • Final Audit

    In the final stage, we rigorously review the client's financial statements, conduct tests to check for changes in accounting and internal controls, and perform selected audit procedures to provide a professional opinion on the accuracy and validity of the financial statements.

Consultation Services

We offer expert guidance and practical solutions to help you navigate complex issues and achieve your business goals.

Overview

Our consultation services offer expert guidance tailored to your specific needs. We provide strategic advice and practical solutions to help you navigate complex issues, optimize performance, and achieve your business goals. Whether you need support with regulatory compliance, operational efficiency, or strategic planning, our experienced consultants are here to assist you in making informed decisions and driving success.

Here are the types of consultations that ATC provide:

Advisory Services:

  • Provide consulting opinions on:

    • VAT system and regulations.
    • Income tax system and regulations.
    • Zakat collection list.
    • Real estate transaction tax system and regulations.
  • Deliver opinions within three working days after receiving all required documents.
  • Consulting services include a set number of consultations; additional ones require extra fees.
  • Opinions and communication are in Arabic and based on submitted documents and data.
  • Excludes technical support for preparing, submitting, or responding to inquiries about tax returns or examinations by ZATCA.

Consultancy Services for VAT Refund:

  • Analyze financial data and supporting documents to verify VAT balance accuracy before legal action.
  • Assist with submitting refund requests to ZATCA for eligible VAT.
  • Provide technical support for responding to authority inquiries and attending discussions related to the refund.
  • Collaborate on tracking the refund process until completion and approval by ZATCA.

Consultancy Services for VAT Refund:

  • Analyze financial data and supporting documents to verify VAT balance accuracy before legal action.
  • Assist with submitting refund requests to ZATCA for eligible VAT.
  • Provide technical support for responding to authority inquiries and attending discussions related to the refund.
  • Collaborate on tracking the refund process until completion and approval by ZATCA.

Consultancy Services for Inspection:

  • Provide advisory opinions and recommendations based on analyses and documents related to ZATCA's examination request.
  • Offer technical support for meeting examination requirements through ZATCA's electronic portal.
  • Collaborate on monitoring examination procedures, responding to inquiries, and attending authority sessions.
  • Assist with preparing and supporting a memorandum of objection if discrepancies are identified, excluding objections to the General Secretariat of Zakat, Taxation, and Customs Committees.

Consultancy Services for Objections:

  • Provide consultative opinions and recommendations based on analyses and documents before submitting an objection to ZATCA rejections.
  • Offer technical support for fulfilling objection requirements via the General Secretariat's electronic portal.
  • Collaborate on monitoring objection proceedings, responding to inquiries, and attending hearing sessions with your legal representative (legal representation not included unless specifically agreed upon).

Technical Support for Accounting Registration:

  • Record accounting entries based on provided documents, either on-site or remotely.
  • Entries are recorded only in Arabic.
  • Provide recommendations for necessary adjustments in the accounting and documentary cycle, if applicable, along with technical support for implementing those adjustments upon request.
  • Deliver program outputs (entries, general ledger, trial balance) in Excel and PDF formats; program copy not provided.
  • Provide support and supervision for organizing or attaching documents to accounting entries if requested.

Consultancy Services for Evaluating Internal Control Systems :

  • Analyze organizational structure to assess authority limits and data flow.
  • Evaluate systems and regulations for asset protection and effectiveness.
  • Examine accounting principles for compliance with International Accounting Standards.

  • Review documentary cycles, identify control points, and test effectiveness, including:

    • Customer debt and credit limit management.
    • Cash flow monitoring through inventory checks and policy review.
    • Stock movement and departmental expenditures.
    • Budget comparisons and variance analysis.
  • Review and match job descriptions, tasks, and authority with actual duties.
  • Provide a report with key observations and recommendations for improvement.
  • Note: Report is for internal use only and not certified by a licensed accountant.

Consulting Services for Studying Transfer Pricing:

  • Review transfer pricing between your company and group companies for local file preparation according to Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority guidelines.
  • Prepare the main file in compliance with transfer pricing instructions for submission upon request.
  • Draft reports in English, discuss with your company, and issue final reports in Arabic and English.
  • Provide all consulting services through remote communication tools.

Consultancy Services for Electronic Invoicing Integration:

  • Verify application compatibility with electronic invoicing regulations.
  • Hold meetings with financial and IT departments to discuss requirements and implementation.
  • Submit a detailed report with findings and recommendations.
  • Note: No technical support for accounting or invoicing programs.
  • Conduct simulation on the “Fatoora Platform” to correct errors before final connection.
