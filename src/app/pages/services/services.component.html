Zakat and Tax services

ATC offers top quality Zakat and Tax services tailored to optimize your business's financial well-being.

Overview

We offer comprehensive zakat and tax services in compliance with Saudi Arabian regulations. Our services include auditing and submitting returns, handling tax and zakat inspections, managing appeals, assisting with refund claims, and providing expert consultations to ensure compliance and maximize tax advantages for our clients.

1. Auditing and submitting zakat and tax returns

  • We audit zakat and tax returns to determine its compliance with the applicable systems requirements in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for each tax and zakat period before submitting the zakat and tax returns to ZATCA through auditing a sample of the documents, financial data, accounts, trial balance and financial statements provided by the client for giving any comments or observation on them.
  • We provide the client with our most significant observations and recommendations before submitting zakat and tax returns to ZATCA to take the corrective procedures (if any).
  • We provide the technical support services pertaining to uploading the zakat and tax return on the ZATCA e-system portal.
  • We provide the client with any updates on the zakat and tax e-system and instructions or guidelines issued by the ZATCA.
  • We provide all consultation services via remote communication programs or at the client's premises.

2. Tax & zakat inspection

  • When ZATCA requires the inspection of zakat or tax return, our team studies the request, reviews the analyses, data, documents and accounts required for the inspection for giving advisory opinion on it before submitting it to ZATCA.
  • We provide technical support for uploading the inspection requirements on ZATCA e-system portal.
  • We follow-up the inspection, respond to the Authority's inquiries, attend the sessions held by the inspection committee till finalizing the inspection procedures and issuing the final evaluation notices.
  • We provide our recommendations to the client in light of the work performed during the inspection by ZATCA in order to take the adequate procedures and perform certain amendments that will positively affect the subsequent inspections.

3. Appeals

  • When the client requests to appeal on ZATCA assessments, in case the result of the inspection performed by ZATCA revealed due amounts on the client as inspection differences according to the submitted documents and data, our team verifies the client's eligibility to appeal and gives the advisory opinion, during the appeal period at ZATCA.
  • We Provide the technical and advisory support in preparing the appeal memo on the amounts that the client desires to appeal on, attached with the financial documents and data after being audited by our team in order to submit the appeal to ZATCA.
  • We provide the technical support services to submit the appeal to ZATCA.
  • In case the appeal inspection by ZATCA was wholly or partially rejected, we provide the technical support to upload the appeal requirements through the e-system portal of the General Secretariat of Zakat, Tax and Customs Committees and follow-up on the appeal procedures and respond to all inquiries and attend hearings with the client's legal representative in accordance with the applicable regulations.

4. Due zakat and tax amounts refund to the client from ZATCA

  • We review a sample of the documents and financial data supporting the amounts due to the client at ZATCA in order to verify the validity of the client's eligibility in such due amounts before taking any procedures with ZATCA.
  • We provide the full technical support in uploading the refund request on the e-system portal of ZATCA for the client to have his due amounts refunded from ZATCA.
  • We respond to all inquiries and requirements of ZATCA and attend the discussion sessions within the scope of the refund request submitted to ZATCA.
  • We follow-up the refund process with ZATCA till finalizing the procedures and issuing the approval on refunding the due amounts to the client.

5. Tax and zakat consultations

  • We provide the advisory opinion in light of the discussions with the client and studying the documents and financial data that are submitted and presented by the client and give the advisory opinion in accordance with the applicable regulations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia as follows:
    • - Zakat levy regulations.
    • - Income tax system and its executive regulations.
    • - Value added tax system and its executive regulations.
    • - Withholding tax provisions - income tax system and its executive regulations.
    • - Real estate tax system transactions and its executive regulations.
    • - Excise goods tax system.
  • We provide the full technical support in uploading the refund request on the e-system portal of ZATCA for the client to have his due amounts refunded from ZATCA.
  • We respond to all inquiries and requirements of ZATCA and attend the discussion sessions within the scope of the refund request submitted to ZATCA.
  • We follow-up the refund process with ZATCA till finalizing the procedures and issuing the approval on refunding the due amounts to the client.

Auditing Services

Our Auditing Services provide insight into the financial health of organizations

Overview

Our Auditing Services offer comprehensive solutions, including audit and internal audit. We ensure compliance with recognized accounting principles, assess risks, and provide technical support. Our services are designed to enhance financial transparency and help clients make informed decisions for sustainable growth.

1. Audit

  • In light of the ongoing globalization, the business environment in which the client's entity operates is becoming more sophisticated. This includes the new principles, legislations and cultures. This, in turn, becomes more burdensome to comply with these systems, legislations and principles. Furthermore, the accelerating competition places additional pressure on the client's business to improve its productivity.
  • The basic aspect of auditing is to verify the correctness and fair presentation of the financial statements and to assure that they are free of material errors.
  • Our audit approach is to provide reasonable assurance about whether the financial statements preparation comply with the recognized accounting principles and that they are free of material errors in classification, figures, or misstatement.
  • Risk assessment is a key factor in our approach. We assess and identify the potential risks including their nature, level and calculate their material value (if possible). As we emphasize on the risks, an added value for the client with regard to auditing is realized.

2. Internal audit

  • Compliance with the systems is a normal extension to the governance obligations which are the responsibility of the senior managements and boards of the companies. Moreover, the nature of good governance assures that the systems followed are aligned to the various activities of the company in addition to the plans and guidelines of the risk departments. This will be an added value to the company and the potential client. The main objective of rendering this service is to ensure that the essence of applying the systems and adhering to them is given the care it deserves in all aspects and activities of the company. Additionally, we are ready to offer support and assistance in this regard through assessments, adequate field inspections to assess the applied governance level in the company and the current risks as well as the potential losses, as a first step. Subsequently, we provide recommendations to the process and handle any issues that may negatively affect the client.
  • Internal audit is an essential internal activity to most of the entities of all sizes, as it supports and assists both the management and board members. The internal auditors, nowadays, experience huge responsibilities as well as face important and sensitive issues relevant to their roles in their enterprises and their responsibilities towards others. Owing to our experience in establishing and rendering internal auditing to various companies and authorities, we are delighted to provide our internal audit services as a contractual service to the client with outstanding professional standards locally and internationally.

3. Ad hoc reports

  • The agreed-upon procedures assignment may require an auditor to undertake procedures with certain audit procedures for specific items for financial data, financial statement or even a complete set of financial data, agreed upon by the auditor and the client and any adequate third party, and upon preparing financial reports with actual results.
  • We undertake the agreed-upon procedures in accordance with “ethical standards of professional accountants” issued by the International Federation of Accountants as per the determined agreed upon terms of these assignments.
  • Our professional team has an extensive experience in preparing ad hoc reports submitted to the competent authorities that require certain procedures to be provided to the competent governmental and non-governmental authorities in order to enhance the credibility in the business sector environment.

Transfer Pricing & Double taxation treaty

Overview

Our Transfer Pricing services include the preparation of local and master files in compliance with ZATCA guidelines, handled by a team of ADIT-certified experts. We also provide technical support for utilizing double taxation treaties, helping clients maximize benefits from international tax agreements.

1. Transfer Pricing

  • We prepare the transfer pricing local file on the inter-company transactions through a team with extensive experience in the field of transfer pricing. This team has a number of ADIT (Advanced Diploma in International Taxation) holders. ADIT is a professional certificate in the field of international taxation and is supervised by the Chartered Institute of Taxation (CIOT), England.
  • We study the transfer pricing methods used between your company and the group companies (inter-company transactions) for the purpose of preparing the local file in accordance with the transfer pricing guidelines issued as per the decision of ZATCA Board No. (19-1-6) dated 25/5/1440 Hijri in preparation for submitting it to ZATCA upon request.
  • We also prepare the master file in accordance with the transfer pricing guidelines issued as per the decision of ZATCA Board No. (19-1-6) dated 25/5/1440 Hijri in preparation for submitting it to ZATCA upon request.

2. Double taxation treaty

  • We provide the technical support to take the procedures required to apply the double taxation treaties concluded between the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the non-resident States - if any.
  • We study the transactions between the client and non-resident companies belonging to other States and determine the advantages that could be obtained through applying the double taxation treaties concluded between the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and these States.
  • We identify the documents and procedures required to benefit from the advantages granted by double taxation treaties, including submitting refund requests according to the mechanism regulated by the ZATCA and in light of the tax brackets determined by the double taxation treaties.

Electronic Invoicing

Overview

Our Electronic Invoicing service ensures the client's systems comply with ZATCA regulations for electronic invoicing. We assess and provide recommendations on system compatibility, conduct simulations on the "Fatora Platform," and offer technical support to ensure seamless integration for tax compliance

Electronic Invoicing

  • We verify the compatibility of the client's application for the first phase (the electronic issuance and saving phase) of electronic invoicing with the “controls, requirements, technical specifications and procedural rules required to implement the provisions of the electronic invoicing regulations” issued by virtue of the decision of the ZATCA Governor No. (62738) dated 23/11/1443 Hijri.
  • We hold meetings with the Financial Department to clarify the requirements of the electronic invoicing regulations and the value added tax system executive regulations and the mechanisms for applying this in the assessment and integration phase as per the company activity, and give our opinion and present our point of view and vision on the extent to which the outputs of the accounting system and the electronic invoicing system are compatible with the mechanisms of preparing the tax return and tax inspection procedures by ZATCA in the future.
  • We submit a detailed report indicating the results of our study of the mechanisms for applying the assessment and integration phase. The report includes our most important observations - if any - and our recommendations to correct any deficiencies in the accounting system and the electronic invoicing system.
  • The scope of our services includes technical support and technical solutions for accounting system and electronic invoicing system, and this service is provided through a competent third party.
  • We conduct a simulation on “Fatora Platform” of the ZATCA before the official commencement of the final assessment with ZATCA.
