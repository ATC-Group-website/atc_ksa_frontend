Company History

Founded in 1997, ATC Group Middle East - Ashraf Abdel Ghani Accountants and Tax Consultants has evolved into a premier tax consultancy firm, recognized for its excellence and leadership in the MENA region. With headquarters in Egypt, we have rapidly expanded our presence across the Middle East, including the KSA and UAE, reflecting our commitment to providing exceptional service and achieving operational excellence. As a proud member of the Geneva Group International (GGI), we are part of a global network of independent accounting firms, business consultants, and legal advisors, which strengthens our ability to offer world-class consultancy services.
