Company History

Established in 2018, ATC Sobol Al-Khebra has quickly grown to become a trusted name in the field of tax and zakat consultancy, recognized for its expertise and dedication to operational excellence Saudi Arabia. As a branch of ATC Group Ashraf Abdel Ghani Accountants and Tax Consultants, the main headquarters is based in Egypt, with a strong presence across the Middle East and North Africa. The group also operates a branch in the UAE, solidifying its position as a leading consultancy firm across the region.

Founded in 1997, ATC Group Ashraf Abdel Ghani Accountants and Tax Consultants has evolved into a premier tax consultancy firm, recognized for its excellence and leadership in the MENA region. With headquarters in Egypt , we rapidly expanded our presence across the Middle East, including the KSA and UAE, and launched ATC Ashraf Abdel Ghani - Sobol Al-Khebra branches in Saudi Arabia in 2019, reflecting our commitment to providing exceptional service and achieving operational excellence.
